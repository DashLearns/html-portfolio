Dash Discusses Film

These movies will blow you away!

"Enter The Dragon"

This is arguably Bruce Lees's greatest film. Not only are the fight scenes amazing, if you look hard enough, you can see Bruce fighting a young Jackie Chan and a young Sammo Hung! Epic.


Aliens

What's more badass than an alien movie? Sigorney Weaver fighting an alien queen in a cargo mech suit.


Coming To America

Eddie Murphy has a litany of funny films. This one is special because of how timeless it is. A phenomal cast (RIP James Earl Jones). CTA is insanely funny, but it's comedy with class.
